Instructions:

  1. Move Disks From Tower A To
    Tower C.
  2. You Can't Stack Larger Disks On
    Top Of Smaller Disks.
  3. If You Get All Disks To Column C Stacked In The Right Order,
    You Win!
  4. Good Luck!!!
